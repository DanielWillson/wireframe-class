HackCville

A community and clubhouse that helps students start themselves and their projects HackCville is an entrepreneurial talent accelerator in Charlottesville (Cville), Virginia. We help students of all backgrounds start themselves and their projects. Founded in 2012, HackCville is a 501(c)(3) non-profit led by U.Va. alumni and students.
